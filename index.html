Why it is preferred to use a check letter in addition

If you only use numbers from 0-9 you are limited to 10 symbols in total to encode your internal number result from the algorithm. Now, in the first place this is due to the limitiation of our decimal system that only offers 10 digits. To trespass this limit we can enhance our symbol list by all alphabetical characters from A-Z so that we end up with 36 different symbols: 0-9 and A-Z. In summary it means that our encoding process is even more precise because our check symbol - based now on modulo 36 - repeats less often and still only consists of one single character.
